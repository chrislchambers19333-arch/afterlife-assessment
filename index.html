How to read your matrix

Every skill you were assessed on falls somewhere across two dimensions: how transferable it is to AI (left to right), and how strong you currently are at it (bottom to top). Where those two things intersect determines the right strategy — automate it, accelerate it with AI, defend it as your moat, or develop it as a human capability. Your seven skills are plotted below as dots. Each one tells you exactly what to do next.

Automate

High AI transferability + strong proficiency. Stop doing this manually — let AI own it and reclaim your time.

Accelerate

High AI transferability + weak proficiency. Your highest-ROI zone — AI can close this gap faster than you can alone.

Defend

Low AI transferability + strong proficiency. Your irreplaceable edge. Price around it. Signal it loudly.

Develop

Low AI transferability + weak proficiency. Your competitive risk zone. Only you can close this gap.
